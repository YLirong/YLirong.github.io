About

I explored more about how to make videos and coding in the classroom (Intro Digital Video). My work uses Adobe Premiere to make videos. In addition to this, there is also a programming website. My idea is to add my favorite fairy tale style elements in my design. Therefore, I use animal pandas to represent humans, or in an implicit way, to present what I experience and see in life.

"Camera?", Digital Video, 1:23min

This work is showing a culture issue, my theme is misuse of technology. I compare the two ways to use the camera. The first part of the video shows people enjoying the good life with a camera. The other part, in an implicit way, shows people's privacy violations by cameras.

“Flipbook”, Installation Art, 2min, 2019

The process of creating this work simulates the presentation and installation into the creation. My idea is to grow up, the album represents memory, and I present the work in the form of a book, like a projection into a book. I use the panda as the protagonist to represent humanity.


“Early Internet Memory”, Html & Css Website, 2019

Web Link

I chose the two most impressive activities I used to play on my computer in my childhood. I rebuild my earliest internet memory with the most representative elements of the two.
