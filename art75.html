About

I was influenced by storybooks that I liked as a kid. Telling stories in simple language and telling interesting stories taught a lot of truth. I like to make works that bring joy and energy. My works have video art and website. The idea of my work comes from my exploration of life. I like to add some elements of tales to express what I think and see.

From my inspiration, I like to use animals as protagonists, or make abstract works, which implicitly represent something. In the first work, I used an abstract method to show a social problem, reducing the sense of horror and making the work hit the point. The second work is growth. I use a childish approach to show that life is beautiful. For the last two works, I also made them cheerful.

"Camera?", Digital Video, 1:23min

This work is showing a culture issue, my theme is misuse of technology. I compare the two ways to use the camera. The first part of the video shows people enjoying the good life with a camera. The other part, in an implicit way, shows people's privacy violations by cameras.

“Flipbook”, Installation Art, 2min, 2019

The process of creating this work simulates the presentation and installation into the creation. My idea is to grow up, the album represents memory, and I present the work in the form of a book, like a projection into a book. I use the panda as the protagonist to represent humanity.


“Early Internet Memory”, Html & Css Website, 2019

Web Link

I chose the two most impressive activities I used to play on my computer in my childhood. I rebuild my earliest internet memory with the most representative elements of the two.

“90's”, Digital Video, 1:11min, 2019

I recall my childhood by linking the three games I used to play as children, and a common thing in them, the grid. It's also refers to 90 ’s game, which people are born 1990-99, they can feel familiar.
