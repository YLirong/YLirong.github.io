 portfolio artist's statement

“Piggy Robot”, Exquisite & Glitch Art, 2019

This work is first made in Photoshop with different images to create an Exquisite. Then mess up the encoding of this image in Audacity, and add the echo effect. I try to add cute elements in this abstract art to create a pleasant feeling in the artwork.

“Moon's Music ”, Composite Art,2019

This is a digital image. I created a hyper-real classic music world. I show my understanding of classical music is elegant and warm in this work. I specifically found three pictures on the internet, fairy elves, sparkling rivers, and style buildings. Then composited in Photoshop.

“Emoji ”, Digital Image, 2019

This is a vector image. Today’s Emoji play an important role in texting. I couldn't find the food I like in the category, so I chose to do this. I rendered a picture I found on the internet in illustrator and made it look like an emoticon.

“Signal”, Digital Sound, 1 min, 2019

This soundtrack combined three sound sources. I recorded the sounds around me. Next, use Audacity to combine them together. It tells a story that people driving on a bad weather day.

“Raining”, Digital Video, 1:03 min, 2019

I created story based on the soundtrack I made. I took video footages and photos. This tell safe driving in a raining. I edited it in Premiere Pro, make the scene matches the sound.




“Self-portrait”, Net Art, 2019

This is a coding art. I made this website with a friend. It combined our same hobbies. This is in a abstract way of our portrait. We created the website on Atom.

“Space Adventure”, Code Art using p5.js, 2019

The idea is that people go to the Galaxy to travel and explore. Because this project is going to be a surreal world, The background is dreamy. People take a flying(bouncing) sailboat, avoiding some obstacles, a mirage (B&W door), the shooting stars, and a floating planet. Find the right door (pink door) that opens the new world. Move the mouse while clicking the mouse to see the hidden planets and the interchangeable door.


“Fantasy Panda”Composite Art, 2019

This series is hyperreal panda residence. I have added the elements I like to the three pictures, Chinese style - bamboo, panda. The pandas in the middle and right images are anthropomorphic.
